Augie

Augie is a virtual credit card that helps build credit by paying bills, with no interest or hidden fees.

Project Overview

Augie is a virtual credit card that helps users build their credit by paying recurring bills. It reports to major credit bureaus, offers zero-interest payments, flexible options, and rewards points to reduce future bills—all without hidden fees. It’s a great tool for improving credit health.
